✡️ My Journey ✡️

Before stepping into the Tech world, I was at that same old place where atleast once, everyone of you have stood in your lives. And that is Finding the Right Career.

My willingness to do something creative and artistic initially pushed me towards Mass Communications, that I did from Amity University, Noida. There, I did a radio show, organized an event at the Annual Festival, made a short film, learnt Adobe Photoshop. But I was most passionate about photography. (Visit my photography page)

These 3 years of my graduation, one area that always had my interest was Social Media and I was deeply inspired by the Digital Makreting techniques. That's why I pursued International Marketing from the University of Law, London (Berlin campus).

My creative instinct pushed me one more time this time when I was looking for a job. I worked as a Content Marketing Intern in a Berlin-based startup.

While applying for jobs in digital marketing, I came across HTML, CSS and JavaScript in many job descriptions. On doing some research, I realized that a CS degree is not needed to become a developer.

With Knowledge, Skill and Practice, anyone can do it. Not to forget how insanely creative and intense web development is. I followed my instinct and just went for it.

What started off as an effort to boost my former career turned into a 9 month grind that put me where I am right now -

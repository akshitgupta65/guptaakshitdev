Forkify API

Forkify App is a search engine for recipes.

  • It allows to search for numerous recipes from across the Internet and display results using pagination.
  • It also allows to bookmark your favourite recipes in order to save for later and also add new recipes of your own.
  • You can also change the number of servings and Forkify will update the quantity of ingredients for you.

Mapty App

Mapty App is the perfect storehouse for your workouts.

  • It keeps track of all your cycling and running workouts on a map.
  • It allows you to add new workouts. Along with that, you can also edit or delete existing workouts on a click.
  • You can easily navigate anywhere on the map to your workout by simply clicking on the workout card.

Bankist App

Bankist App is the gateway to Modern Banking.

  • It displays your username, account balance, summary and transactions of your account.
  • It also contains a 5-minute logout timer for security purpose.
  • It allows you to sort transactions, transfer funds, request loan, currency conversions, and more.

Journey

Before stepping into the Tech world, I was at that same old place where everyone has to stand in their lives atleast once. And that is Finding the Right Career.

My willingness to do something creative and artistic initially pushed me towards Mass Communications. There, I did a radio show, organized an event at the Annual Festival, made a short film, learnt Adobe Photoshop, but Photography was something that I was most passionate about.
Visit my photography page ➡️

At that time, Social Media was something that always held my interest. I was deeply inspired by Digital Makreting. That's why I decided to pursue International Marketing.

My creative instinct pushed me again when I was looking for a job. I worked as a Content Marketing Intern where I created content for blogs and newsletters.
Open Resume ↘️

While applying for jobs in digital marketing, I came across HTML, CSS and JavaScript in many job descriptions. On doing some research, I realized I don't need a CS degree to become a developer.

With knowledge, skill and practice, anyone can do it. Not to forget how insanely creative and intense web development is. I followed my instinct and just went for it.

What started off as an effort to boost my former career turned into a 1-year grind that put me where I am right now -

A Frontend Web Developer looking for opportunities to kick-start my career

One of the key ingredients in becoming a competent web developer is continuous learning and increasing our knowledge database. It is pivotal for a budding developer to be familiar with industry standards and must have the ability to work with popular libraries and frameworks.

My next step is to learn jQuery. Another high-in-demand library that I am really looking forward to is React. Finally, to learn about designing resposive webpages, I would master Bootstrap.

That's why I have started a course on freeCodeCamp.org that is not only teaching me everything I need at this stage, but also giving me projects to work and learn.
